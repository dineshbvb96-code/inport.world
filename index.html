About Us

Inport Marine Supply is a trusted ship chandler based in India, providing high-quality marine provisions, frozen supplies, bonded stores, and cabin & deck stores to vessels calling at Indian ports. We ensure timely delivery, competitive pricing, and reliable service for ship owners, managers, and crews.
