About Us

Inport Marine Supply is a professional ship chandler based in India, supplying high-quality marine provisions, bonded stores, frozen supplies, and cabin & deck stores to vessels calling at Indian ports. We focus on timely delivery, consistent quality, and competitive pricing to meet the expectations of ship owners, managers, and crew.
